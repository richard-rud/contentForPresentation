Why data visualization?

Vision trumps all other senses. We learn and remember best through pictures, not through written or spoken words.

Why interactive?

Static visualizations can only offer pre-composed “views” of data. The number of dimensions are therefore limited, when all visual elements must be present on the same surface at the same time.

Why on the web?

Visualizations aren’t truly visual unless they are seen. Getting your work out there for others to see is critical, and publishing on the web is the quickest way to reach a global audience.

What I used for data visualization

D3 loads, binds, transforms and transitions data using web technologies.
